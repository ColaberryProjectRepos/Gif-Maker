GIF Overview

Gif's are the preferred document style for Colaberry Network.  Instead of taking tons of screenshots, you will instead put together a short video (Gif) so the user can see the motion of the step vs using a ton of screenshots.



There are many Gif Builders out there.  This project will highlight the ones that are used the most.  Each step will highlight a different Gif maker.  Feel free to leave comments on your experience each of them.



Rules: 

Time:  <= 30 seconds.

Reason: It confuses the user because they don't know where to start


Delay: Allow delay at beginning or end

Reason: Without delay it's hard to know when the video starts and when it ends since it just repeats
